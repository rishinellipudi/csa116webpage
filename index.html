How the Riddle Works

The riddle follows a simple algebraic sequence, which always results in the same number, regardless of your starting number. Let's see the steps:

  1. Choose any number

  2. Double it

  3. Add 6

  4. Divide it by half

  5. Subtract the number you started with

After these steps, you’ll always end up with 3 no matter what number you started with.
